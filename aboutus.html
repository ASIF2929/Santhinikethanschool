About Us

Santhinikethan High school; was founded in 1996 with the vision of delivering quality education that fosters academic excellence, leadership, and personal growth. Our school serves students from Nursery (Kindergarten) to Class 10, offering a safe, nurturing, and modern learning environment.

Our Vision

Our aim is to bring out the best in each child to fulfill their potential both in academic and social human terms. We follow the effective teaching method hich has been proven highly succesfull in recognising the individual children development

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a balanced and inclusive curriculum that fosters academic excellence and creative thinking, while encouraging active participation in sports, arts, and cultural activities, and nurturing moral values, empathy, and leadership in every student. Cultivate moral values, empathy, and leadership.

At Santhinikethan, "Every Child Matters, and Every Dream Counts."

Founder – Mr. M.Hari Kumar Reddy

Mr. Hari Kumar Reddy is an educationist and visionary who laid the foundation of Santhinikethan High School with a mission to make quality education accessible to all. Under his leadership continues to guide the institution.

Correspondent – Mr. M.Prathap Kumar Reddy

Mr . M. Prathap Kumar Reddy ensures smooth academic operations and student well-being at Santhinikethan. With a warm personality and a commitment to discipline and excellence, he plays a key role in school development and parent engagement.

We believe in holistic development through a blend of academics, co-curricular activities, and character-building programs. Our experienced faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure make Santhinikethan a leading choice for value-based education.
