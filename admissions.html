Welcome to Santhinikethan High School,

We’re delighted that you’re considering joining our family of learners. Here’s how to get started:

Who Can Apply?

We welcome admissions from:

  •  Pre-primary (Nursery, LKG, UKG).
  •  Classes I to X.

Admission Process

    1️⃣ Visit Our School:
  •   Meet our teachers and explore our facilities. Get a feel for our vibrant learning environment.
    • 2️⃣ Collect the Application Form:
  •   Available at the school office during working hours or you can download from here
    • 3️⃣ Fill & Submit:
  •   Complete the application form with all required details.
  •   Submit the form along with the following documents:
  •   Birth certificate
  •   Previous school’s transfer certificate (if applicable)
  •   Study Certificate
  •   AADHAR Card
  •   Passport-size photographs
    • 4️⃣ Entrance/Interaction (for specific classes):
  •   For classes I and above, there may be a short interaction or assessment to understand the child’s academic level.
  •   For competitive exam coaching (Sainik, Navodaya, RMS), separate guidance and practice tests are available.
    • 5️⃣ Confirmation & Fee Payment:
  •   For competitive exam coaching (Sainik, Navodaya, RMS), separate guidance and practice tests are available.
