Welcome to Santhinikethan High School, Kalikiri, Andhra Pradesh!

"Where every child’s dream takes flight and every talent shines."

At Santhinikethan High School, we believe that education is more than just textbooks and exams—it’s about nurturing every child’s potential, fostering a sense of community, and preparing them for a bright future.

"Knowledge is Light, Character is Strength."

A Message from Our Founding Visionary

“For me, equality is important. There should be no divisions—no poor or rich students—every child deserves the same opportunities. Discipline is equally important, as it shapes character and builds a strong foundation for life.”
— M.Harikumar Reddy, Founder (1996)

Since 1996, these values have been the cornerstone of our school community—ensuring that every student feels valued, respected, and empowered to grow.

A Message from Our Correspondent

“At Santhinikethan, I believe that education must go hand-in-hand with sports, arts, and cultural activities. I always encourage our students to participate enthusiastically in everything they love to do—and I love seeing their excitement every day!”
— M.Pratap Kumar Reddy, Correspondent

Why Choose Santhinikethan?

  • Equality & Inclusion: Every child is treated with equal respect and opportunity—no divisions, no barriers.
  • Balanced Learning: Academic excellence enriched with sports, arts, and cultural activities.
  • Dedicated Educators: Teachers who guide, inspire, and mentor each student.
  • Discipline & Values: We cultivate discipline alongside creativity to build responsible, compassionate individuals.
